GAME OF THE WEEK

Chicago vs Houston

Two young talented Quarterbacks face off in the Sunday night primetime slot. These two offenses are potent and filled with playmakers ready to put up points. This game promises to be an explosive showdown!

Kansas City vs Cincinnati

This will be a star-studded matchup with Joe Burrow and Ja’Marr Chase facing off against Patrick Mahomes and Travis Kelce. Every time these two teams meet, fans are in for a treat. Look for this one to come down to the wire!

New Orleans vs Dallas

Two of the top teams in their divisions square off in what is expected to be an exceptionally good game. The Saints look to build on a very impressive Week 1 performance, while the Cowboys rely on their stout defense to shut down this red-hot Saints offense.
